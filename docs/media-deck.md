# QuoteCheck Home Media Deck

## Slide 1: Hook

**Three Contractor Quotes. Three Totally Different Prices. Now What?**

Homeowners routinely face expensive repair and renovation decisions with little confidence. A vague or incomplete quote can lead to surprise costs, bad deposits, missing cleanup, permit issues, or painful change orders.

## Slide 2: Problem

**The Current Tools Stop Too Early**

Cost guides show average prices. Contractor marketplaces help users find more pros. But homeowners still need help comparing the quotes they already received, spotting missing scope details, and knowing what to ask before signing.

## Slide 3: Solution

**QuoteCheck Home**

A one-page guided quote comparison tool. Users manually enter structured bid details, then receive a clear risk report, missing-item checklist, price-spread summary, and copy-paste contractor clarification scripts.

## Slide 4: Roadmap

**Build the Narrow Tool First**

MVP: structured manual quote wizard, comparison report, disclaimers, print-to-PDF, and pay-per-report unlock.

Later: saved projects, family review mode, change-order tracker, renovation packet export, and contractor communication log.
