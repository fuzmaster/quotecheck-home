Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

QuoteCheck Home is a single-page tool that runs in your browser. Everything you type into the wizard — your ZIP code, project type, room description, contractor names, prices, timelines, and free-text notes — is stored only in your browser's localStorage. It is never sent to our servers.

  • Project basics (ZIP, project type, area, urgency, permit likelihood)
  • Contractor names, total prices, deposit amounts, timelines, material quality, notes
  • Your scope-checklist answers
  • The generated comparison report

What we send to our servers

The only information that ever leaves your browser to a server we operate is an opaque reportId (a random UUID) when you start a checkout or verify a Stripe session. We do not store this reportId anywhere.

Payments (Stripe)

If you choose to purchase the premium report (when available), payment is handled entirely by Stripe. Stripe collects your email address and payment details on its own servers under its own privacy policy. We never see your card number. We receive a payment-confirmation webhook from Stripe containing only a session ID and the opaque reportId.

Analytics & cookies

We do not set tracking cookies. We do not run third-party analytics. Stripe Checkout sets its own cookies on the Stripe domain when you visit it.

Deletion & export

Because everything lives in your browser, you can erase all QuoteCheck data by clearing your browser storage for this site (in your browser settings under "Site data" or "Cookies and other site data"). To request deletion of any Stripe-side payment record, see Stripe's privacy controls.

Disclosure

QuoteCheck Home is an educational decision-support tool. It does not provide legal, construction, engineering, insurance, or financial advice. It does not verify contractor accuracy, detect fraud, or certify construction scope.
